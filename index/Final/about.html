Our Story

Video Game Console Company was founded in 1990 by a group of passionate gamers who wanted to create the best video game consoles on the market. They believed that gaming should be accessible to everyone, and set out to create consoles that were affordable and easy to use, without sacrificing quality or performance.

Over the years, Video Game Console Company has become a leader in the video game industry, with a dedicated team of developers and designers who are constantly pushing the boundaries of technology and innovation. Our consoles are known for their reliability, speed, and high-quality graphics, and our exclusive games have become household names.

Our Values

  • Quality: We believe in creating products that are built to last, with the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
  • Innovation: We are constantly looking for new ways to improve our products and stay ahead of the competition.
  • Creativity: We believe that gaming is an art form, and are dedicated to creating games that are as beautiful as they are fun to play.
  • Inclusivity: We believe that gaming should be for everyone, regardless of age, gender, or background.
  • Community: We are committed to building a strong community of gamers who share our passion for video games.
